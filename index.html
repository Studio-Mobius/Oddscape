About

Studio Oddscape provides terrain analysis, LiDAR interpretation, and landscape intelligence for sites where landform, water behaviour, and long-term change matter. Working across estates, coastal zones, and complex landholdings, Oddscape reveals patterns in elevation, drainage, erosion, and terrain structure that are not immediately visible on the ground.

The result is a clearer understanding of how land behaves — supporting informed decisions, reduced risk, and better use of terrain before intervention begins.

Site Insight Options

A structured understanding of land before design, construction, or investment begins. Each level of insight is aligned to the scale, complexity, and strategic importance of the site.

£95 — Starter Insight
A concise LiDAR-based terrain overview with a clear plain-English reading of the land.

Suitable for initial site understanding and early-stage feasibility.
£150–£300 — Site Insight Report
Detailed terrain analysis including elevation modelling, slope behaviour, and drainage interpretation, with site-specific commentary.

Supports planning thinking, design direction, and early decision-making.
£500+ — Advanced Terrain Analysis
Comprehensive site intelligence with layered mapping, terrain interpretation, and analysis of underlying landscape processes.

Designed for larger land areas, estates, and more complex environmental conditions.
