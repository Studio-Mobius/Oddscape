About

Studio Oddscape provides terrain analysis and LiDAR-based landscape intelligence to support understanding of landform, water behaviour, and long-term environmental change.

Working across estates, coastal environments, and complex landholdings, Oddscape supports the interpretation of landscape character and terrain processes — identifying patterns in elevation, drainage, and erosion that are not immediately visible on the ground.

This provides a clear, evidence-based foundation for informed decisions, reduced risk, and more effective and responsible use of land before intervention.

Terrain Intelligence

A structured understanding of land before design, construction, or investment. Each level of analysis is aligned to the scale, complexity, and strategic importance of the site.

For most sites, detailed analysis provides the clearest basis for decision-making.

Professional Approach

Studio Oddscape applies data-led analysis to support landscape planning, design, and land management.

Work is informed by principles of landscape character, environmental sensitivity, and long-term change, ensuring that decisions respond to both physical processes and place-based context.

This approach aligns with established landscape practice, supporting sustainable outcomes across rural, coastal, and estate environments.
