Terrain insight for land, coast, and landscape decisions

About

Studio Oddscape provides terrain analysis, LiDAR interpretation, and landscape insight for sites where landform, drainage, and ground conditions matter. By revealing patterns in elevation, erosion, water movement, and terrain form, Oddscape helps turn complex land into clear, usable understanding for gardens, coastal sites, and environmental projects.

Site Insight Options

A clearer understanding of land before design, construction, or investment begins. Each level is shaped to suit the scale, complexity, and ambition of the site.

£95 — Starter Insight
A concise LiDAR-based terrain overview with a plain-English summary of the site.

Suitable for early understanding of gardens, plots, and smaller areas.
£150–£300 — Site Insight Report
Detailed terrain analysis including elevation, slope, drainage patterns, and site-specific interpretation.

Suitable for design decisions, planning thinking, and practical problem-solving.
£500+ — Advanced Terrain Analysis
A more comprehensive site reading with layered mapping, terrain interpretation, and deeper analysis of landscape processes and opportunity.

For complex sites, larger land areas, or professional use.
