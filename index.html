About

Studio Oddscape works beneath the surface of land.

Using LiDAR, terrain modelling, and visual analysis, it reveals how landscapes actually behave — how water moves, where pressure builds, and how change unfolds over time.

Across estates, coastlines, and complex landholdings, the focus is on what isn’t immediately visible — the hidden structure that shapes risk, opportunity, and long-term stability.

The outcome is clear, grounded understanding.

Less assumption.

More certainty.

Better decisions before intervention.

Professional Context

Member of the Landscape Institute since 2014.

Over a decade of independent coastal observation, mapping, and terrain analysis.

Terrain Intelligence

Terrain Intelligence is a way of seeing land properly before anything is done to it.

It combines LiDAR data, terrain modelling, and visual analysis to understand how a site truly behaves — not how it appears on plans.

Analysis scales to the site — from broad landscape patterns to detailed terrain behaviour — revealing risk, constraint, and opportunity early.

No design bias.
No assumptions.
Just the land, understood clearly.
