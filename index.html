What I Do

Terrain Modelling

High-resolution terrain models generated from LiDAR datasets, prepared for analysis, visualisation, and 3D workflows.

Coastal Analysis

Reading landscape change through elevation, erosion patterns, and time-based spatial data.

Visual Outputs

Blender-based renders and map outputs that communicate terrain clearly — for both technical and public audiences.

Why It Matters

Landscapes are not static — they shift, erode, settle, and reform over time. Most data captures a moment. Oddscape explores the movement behind it.

By translating terrain into readable form, complex environments become understandable, visible, and usable.
