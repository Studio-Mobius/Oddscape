What Oddscape Does

Terrain Modelling

High-resolution terrain models generated from LiDAR datasets, prepared for analysis, visualisation, and 3D workflows.

Coastal Analysis

Coastal landscapes interpreted through elevation, erosion patterns, and time-based spatial data.

Visual Outputs

Blender-based renders and mapped outputs that communicate terrain clearly — for both technical and public audiences.

Why It Matters

Landscapes are not static — they shift, erode, settle, and reform over time. Most datasets capture a moment. Oddscape explores the movement behind it.

Through translation into readable form, complex terrain becomes visible, understandable, and usable.

Approach

Oddscape operates between analysis and interpretation — not just processing terrain, but revealing the character of place through form, data, and time.
