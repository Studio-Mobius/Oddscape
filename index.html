About

Studio Oddscape provides terrain analysis, LiDAR interpretation, and landscape intelligence for sites where landform, water behaviour, and long-term change are critical to decision-making.

Working across estates, coastal environments, and complex landholdings, Oddscape reveals underlying terrain structure — identifying patterns in elevation, drainage, and erosion that are not immediately visible on the ground.

This provides the basis for informed decisions, reduces risk, and supports more effective use of land before intervention begins.

Terrain Intelligence Options

A structured understanding of land before design, construction, or investment begins. Each level of analysis is aligned to the scale, complexity, and strategic importance of the site.

For most sites, a detailed analysis provides the clearest basis for decision-making.
